4pm – 11pm

Fairmont Banff Springs
405 Spray Avenue, Banff, Canada

The wedding will take place at the Fairmont Banff Springs, located inside Banff National Park, nestled within the Canadian Rockies at the base of Rundle Mountain. The reception is black tie, but feel free to sport granola gorpcore attire throughout the week.











Itinerary
October 14th, 2025
Lake Agnes Tea House Hike

The Lake Agnes Tea House, situated near Lake Louise at an altitude of 7,000 ft, was originally built in 1901 by the Canadian Pacific Railway as a refuge for hikers, and started serving tea in 1905. The hike up to the Lake Agnes Tea House is well-suited for all ability levels.





October 15th, 2025
Sundance Loop Ride

Ride up natural sulphur hot springs as we make our way to one of Banff’s national historic sites: the Cave and Basin. Next, we'll trek along the tranquil Bow River and take in magnificent views of the Bow Valley before pressing through thick marshes as we climb Sulphur Mountain. Finally, we’ll continue up to Windy Knoll to take in the sweeping landscape.
